Regional Trails Inventory

The Regional Trails Inventory includes all off-road trails in DVRPC's nine-county Greater Philadelphia region. The inventory captures walking, hiking, multi-use, and biking trails, including trails outside of the Circuit Trails network. The inventory is intended for planning purposes and was compiled using several sources including data from county planners, open data portals, PDF park and trail maps available online, Google Maps' trails layer, and Strava's Global Heatmap. Trails were manually verified and corrected using leaf-off aerial imagery and other sources of reasonable reliability.

Find out more about the Regional Trails Program and trail planning at DVRPC.

Surface Material Type

Crushed Stone/Gravel - An improved surface consisting of packed stone material.
Dirt - An unimproved or lightly improved surface consisting mainly of local material.
Grass - An unimproved or lightly improved surface consisting of natural ground cover (usually mowed).
Paved - An improved surface consisting of asphalt or concrete.
Stone Dust - An improved surface consisting of packed fine stone particles (a.k.a. stone fines).
Sand/Sandy Soil - An unimproved or lightly improved surface consisting of sand or sand-like soils.
Varies - More than one of the above surface types.
Unverified - Surface type was not verified.
